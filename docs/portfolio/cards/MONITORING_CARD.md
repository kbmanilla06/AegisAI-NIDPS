# Monitoring and Feedback Card

- **Source:** accepted synthetic/offline artifacts only
- **Output:** aggregate quality, drift, sample-sufficiency, task, and feedback
  evidence
- **Low sample or mismatched provenance:** `not_evaluable`, never zero or green
- **Feedback:** bounded, sanitized, audited observation with immutable evidence
  binding
- **Training/promotion:** no automatic relabeling, retraining, activation, or
  promotion
- **Privacy:** no raw payloads, endpoint lists, model inputs, credentials,
  internal paths, or analyst-note text in aggregate reports

Monitoring cannot create or modify alerts, detections, incidents, or prevention
actions.
